About Evka Consultz

Delivering regulatory excellence across Africa since 2007.

Our Story

Founded in 2007, Evka Consultz Ltd has grown to become a trusted partner for pharmaceutical and healthcare companies navigating the complex regulatory landscapes across Africa.

Our team combines decades of regulatory expertise with deep local knowledge to deliver compliance solutions that ensure safer healthcare products reach patients.

Headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya, we serve clients throughout sub-Saharan Africa with a focus on quality, efficiency, and reliability.
